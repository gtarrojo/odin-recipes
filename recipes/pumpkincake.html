Pumpkin Cake

This recipe for a delicious pumpkin cake is baked in a sheet pan. It's lightly spiced, very moist, and great for potlucks. When cool, top with your favorite cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

  • Flour: All-purpose flour gives the cake batter structure.

  • Leaveners: Baking soda and baking powder acts as leaveners, which means they help the cake rise.

  • Cinnamon: Ground cinnamon lends warmth and flavor.

  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor, but it won’t make the cake taste salty.

  • Sugar: You’ll need two cups of white sugar for this sweet pumpkin cake.

  • Oil: Vegetable oil is essential for this moist cake.

  • Pumpkin: This recipe calls for canned pumpkin, but you can use homemade pumpkin puree.

  • Vanilla: Take the flavor up a notch with a teaspoon of vanilla extract.

  • Eggs: Four eggs add moisture and help bind the batter together.

  • Nuts: Walnuts are optional, but they add welcome crunch. Pecans will also work well.

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl and beat the wet ingredients in another.

  2. Step 2

    Beat the flour mixture into the wet mixture.

  3. Step 3

    Stir in the nuts (if using) and pour it into a prepared sheet pan.

  4. Step 4

    Bake until a toothpick comes out clean.
